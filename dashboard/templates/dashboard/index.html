❤️ we are NORSUnians

The Negros Oriental State University (NORSU) Bayawan–Sta. Catalina Campus has a rich history rooted in the effort to bring accessible education to the southern part of Negros Oriental after World War II. It began on July 5, 1947, as Tolong Junior High School in what is now Bayawan, becoming the first public secondary school outside Dumaguete in the province.
