About

College of Criminal Justice Education

For the past 25 years, the College of Criminal Justice Education continuously brings professional training to students, for them to be intellectually, morally, physically and socially skilled individuals equipped to contribute to global social and professional demands. The CCJE is committed to produce graduates with scientific, artistic, and technological skills that meet global standards to aid in the advantage of society in its interminable quest towards development. The CCJE provides academic and academic-support programs designed for holistic development and professional advances for students in fields of specialization in both the arts and sciences.
