About

College of Arts & Sciences

For the past 25 years, the College of Arts and Sciences continuously brings professional training to students, for them to be intellectually, morally, physically and socially skilled individuals equipped to contribute to global social and professional demands. The CAS is committed to produce graduates with scientific, artistic, and technological skills that meet global standards to aid in the advantage of society in its interminable quest towards development. The CAS provides academic and academic-support programs designed for holistic development and professional advances for students in fields of specialization in both the arts and sciences.
